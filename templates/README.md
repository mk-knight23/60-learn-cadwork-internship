## ðŸŽ¯ How to Use These Templates

1. **Choose the appropriate template** for your deliverable
2. **Copy the template** to your working directory
3. **Fill in the placeholders** (marked with `[BRACKETS]`)
4. **Remove unused sections** to keep it focused
5. **Review against quality standards** (see `/rubrics/`)
6. **Submit for review** following project workflow

---

## ðŸ“ Template Conventions

### Required Sections

Marked with **REQUIRED** - these must be filled in for every deliverable.

### Optional Sections

Marked with **OPTIONAL** - include only if relevant to your work.

### Placeholders

Text in `[SQUARE_BRACKETS]` should be replaced with your specific content.

### Examples

Text in `> blockquotes` are examples to guide your writing.
